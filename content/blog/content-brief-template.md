## The quick answer

A content brief prevents thin content and makes internal linking automatic.

## Template

- Primary keyword + intent
- The “answer first” paragraph
- H2/H3 outline
- 5 internal links to include (see: [Topical authority](/blog/internal-linking-topical-authority))
- FAQ questions
