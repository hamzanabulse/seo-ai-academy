## The quick answer

Great titles are clear, specific, and match intent. Use a few proven patterns and stop overthinking.

## 6 title patterns

- How to + outcome
- Checklist: X items
- The playbook for X
- X vs Y (comparison)
- Best X for Y (buyer intent)
- Mistakes to avoid
