## The quick answer

Stop chasing spam links. Earn links by publishing something journalists can cite.

## The approach

- Create a small dataset (even 50–200 samples)
- Publish the insight + charts
- Pitch the angle to writers in your niche

## Strengthen trust

Links work best with trust pages: [Editorial policy](/editorial-policy) + [E‑E‑A‑T checklist](/blog/eeat-checklist).
