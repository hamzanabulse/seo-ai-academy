## The quick answer

Programmatic SEO works when each template page is genuinely useful and not duplicate.

## 5 templates that work

1) Location pages (City + service)
2) Comparison pages (X vs Y)
3) Alternatives pages
4) Use-case pages
5) Glossary pages

## Avoid the biggest mistake

Thin/duplicated pages hurt site quality. Use: [Technical SEO checklist](/blog/technical-seo-checklist).
