## What E‑E‑A‑T is

E‑E‑A‑T stands for **Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust**. In 2026 it’s how you separate real sites from mass AI content.

## The checklist

### Page-level

- A clear author (real person) with bio
- Evidence of experience (photos, steps, screenshots, “we tested…")
- References when facts matter
- Updated date when content changes

### Site-level

- About page with who/why
- Contact page
- Editorial policy
- Privacy policy

### Content-level

- No thin pages
- No duplicated pages
- Strong internal linking to supporting guides

## Quick win

Pick your top 10 pages and apply the checklist. Then prune or merge the weakest pages.
